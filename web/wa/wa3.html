Introduction

coffee was first introduced in the 15th century,and has a long history within the middle east.

it originated in yemen where they first roasted the beans and brewed coffee similarly to the way we all do now.

its main use was by Sufi circles to stay awake for their religious rituals

Types of Coffee

Generally there are two main coffee types

  • Arabica
  • Robusta

more often arabica is used for its sweeter and smoother flavour,whereas robusta is stronger and more bitter.

robusta is mainly found in vietnam (they are the largest producers of robusta coffee) as well as parts of india and indonesia.

arabica coffee is found almost everywhere and is the main source of coffee for many popular coffee chains.

Best Coffee Spots, Boulder

in my opinion these are a few of the best places in boulder to get a good cup of coffee whether you like it black, sweet, hot, or iced.

  • January Coffee

    they have a variety of choices as well as seasonal drinks, my personal favorite is their dirty horchata latte, areas to sit and study. only downside is theres no wifi.

  • Alpine Modern Cafe

    a perfect place to sit and study or have breakfast/brunch. also good to grab a cup while shopping on 29th st mall.

  • Flatirons Coffee

    good place to sit and study, my favorite part is the names of their drinks are unconventional to say the least. and the decor and overall vibe of the place is very relaxed.
